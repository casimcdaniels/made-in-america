title: ChatGPT and Generative AI
date: "2022-2024"
era: digital
---
The release of ChatGPT and similar generative AI tools sparks widespread discussion about automation in knowledge work. Writers, coders, designers, and other professionals grapple with how AI will change their work. New roles emerge (prompt engineers, AI trainers) while concerns grow about job displacement and the future of creative and technical work.
