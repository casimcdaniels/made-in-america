title: Writers Guild and SAG-AFTRA Strikes
date: "2023"
era: digital
---
Hollywood writers and actors strike for months, demanding protections against AI replacing their work. The strikes result in restrictions on AI-generated scripts and digital replicas, establishing precedents for other creative industries. The conflict highlights growing worker resistance to AI automation across knowledge work sectors.
