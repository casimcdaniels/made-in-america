title: Rise of Social Media Platforms
date: "2004-2010"
era: digital
---
Facebook (2004), YouTube (2005), Twitter (2006), and Instagram (2010) launch, creating entirely new job categories: social media managers, content creators, influencers, and community managers. These platforms transform marketing, communication, and entertainment, while creating new forms of precarious gig work.
