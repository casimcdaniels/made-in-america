title: Creator Economy Boom
date: "2020s"
era: digital
---
Platforms like TikTok, YouTube, and Substack enable content creators to monetize their work directly. Millions of people become full-time or part-time creators, influencers, and online educators. However, income is highly unequal, with most creators earning very little while a small percentage capture most of the revenue.
