title: Gig Economy Expansion
date: "2010s"
era: digital
---
Platforms like Uber (2009), Airbnb (2008), and DoorDash (2013) create millions of gig economy jobs. Workers classified as 'independent contractors' lack traditional employment protections, sparking debates about worker rights, benefits, and the future of work. The gig economy model spreads to knowledge work through platforms like Upwork and Fiverr.
