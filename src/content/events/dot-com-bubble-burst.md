title: Dot-Com Bubble Burst
date: "2000-2002"
era: digital
---
The collapse of the dot-com bubble leads to massive layoffs in the tech industry. Thousands of web developers, programmers, and tech workers lose their jobs as internet startups fail. The crash exposes the precarious nature of tech employment and the volatility of the digital economy.
