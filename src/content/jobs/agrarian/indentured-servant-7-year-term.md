WANTED: Indentured Servants for Tobacco Plantation. The subscriber offers passage to America in exchange for seven years' service. Suitable for able-bodied men and women between the ages of 16 and 25 years who are desirous of improving their condition in the New World.

Terms of indenture: The servant shall labor faithfully for a term of seven years in the cultivation of tobacco, following all lawful commands of the master or his overseer. In return, the master shall provide passage across the Atlantic, adequate provisions (cornmeal and salt pork), lodging, and clothing suitable to the season. The servant shall work from sunup to sundown, six days per week, in the tobacco fields, performing such tasks as planting, weeding, harvesting, and processing the crop.

Upon completion of the indenture term, the servant shall receive freedom dues as customarily provided: a suit of clothes, tools, and possibly a small parcel of land, at the master's discretion. Those interested must sign articles of indenture before a magistrate. The master reserves the right to discipline servants as necessary to maintain order and productivity.
