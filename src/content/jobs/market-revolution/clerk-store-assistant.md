WANTED: A Clerk for a Dry Goods Establishment. A reputable merchant seeks a young man of good character and education to serve as clerk in his store. The applicant must be a free white male, between the ages of 16 and 30 years, preferably 18 to 25, with basic education including reading, writing, and arithmetic.

Duties include: Assisting customers with their purchases; measuring fabrics and weighing goods; calculating prices and making change; keeping accounts and recording sales; maintaining inventory; stocking shelves; and performing such other tasks as may be required. The clerk must be polite, respectful, and trustworthy, as he will handle money and accounts.

Hours are from 8 o'clock in the morning until 8 or 9 o'clock in the evening, six days per week. Wages range from $6 to $10 per week, depending on experience and ability. This position offers young men the opportunity to learn the mercantile trade, though advancement to partnership is rare. The position requires education and literacy, and is therefore suitable only for free white men who have had the benefit of schooling.
