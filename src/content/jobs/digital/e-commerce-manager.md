E-Commerce Manager. Drive online sales and grow our digital presence! We're seeking an experienced E-Commerce Manager to manage our online store operations and digital marketing.

What You'll Do: Manage product listings (descriptions, photos, pricing). Optimize for search engines (SEO, keywords, meta tags). Run digital ads (Google Ads, Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads). Analyze sales data and customer behavior using Google Analytics. Manage inventory and coordinate with warehouse. Write and execute email marketing campaigns. Handle customer service and returns. Manage e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce).

Requirements: Bachelor's degree in marketing, business, or related field. 3+ years e-commerce experience. Experience with Shopify, WooCommerce, or similar platforms. Strong analytical skills (Google Analytics, Excel). Digital marketing experience (SEO, SEM, social media ads). Must be data-driven and metrics-focused. Strong communication and customer service skills. Willingness to work evenings and weekends.

Compensation: $50,000 - $65,000 per year. Location: Remote work available. Benefits: Health insurance. 401(k) matching. Flexible schedule.

Online sales happen 24/7 - must be available to respond to issues. Data-driven role where everything is measured by metrics, KPIs, and ROI.
