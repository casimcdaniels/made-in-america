POSITION AVAILABLE: Data Entry Operator. AT&T seeks women for data entry positions. This is clerical work suitable for women, involving the transition from manual record-keeping to digital systems.

Duties include: Entering data into computer systems using keyboards and terminals; verifying accuracy of entered data; and maintaining records. Operators must be able to work repetitive tasks without complaint and must have excellent typing skills.

Salary is $8,500 per year. Applicants must be women, as this is women's work. A high school diploma is required, along with a typing speed of 60+ words per minute. Previous telephone operator or clerical experience is preferred. Accuracy and attention to detail are essential.

This is entry-level work with no advancement to programming positions. Operators must be willing to work long hours performing repetitive tasks. No men need apply, as this position is for women only.
